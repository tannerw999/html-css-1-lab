A group of penguins in the water is called a raft, but on land they are called a waddle.

The black and white look of penquins is a clever camouflage called countershading

Penquins evolved to fly underwater by having solid bones instead of hollw ones. This reduces the tendency to float.

Penguins and polar bears are not together in the wild. Penguins live south of the equator and polar bears live north of the equator.

Male penguins gift female penquins with rocks in order to woo them. The females use these rocks to help build nests.

Penguins are some of the most streamlined animals in the world.
